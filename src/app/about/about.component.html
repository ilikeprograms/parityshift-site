About us

Parity shift was created as a company to make things easier. In a nutshell. That is the over arching rational for the company.

Theres nothing more annoying than having to repeat the same process over an over again, when this process is not smooth, or takes longer than it should.

At Parity shift, we know how to develop web based products which can help with this. So thats what we do.

We make products which make things less repetitive, less work, so you can focus on what you do best.

Our first product Porthole, came from identifying that a better UI for parts of AdWords would cut down on repetition, and just make that less of a pain point.

The same thing could be said about alot of things.
